LEADING ENGINEERING & MANAGEMENT COLLEGES IN INDIA

Sandip Foundation established in 2005, affiliated to Pune University is the epitome of knowledge across Maharashtra and also well known for its best engineering colleges & management colleges in Nashik. We have created a benchmark in providing world-class programs to educate students of different Field. Our Institute has been ranked in the top 100 colleges in India and thereby managed to attract some of the very prestigious recruiters, and they have built a talent pipeline from Sandip Foundation to fulfil their talent requirements constantly. Our Faculties has set sublime standards for departing knowledge in terms of technical and managerial aspects in the new era of dynamic globalization.

Why Sandip Foundation?

  • It helps students to learn in depth through technological and anatomical models
  • Explore Experiential learning to ensure you are industry ready
  • We bring the best opportunities and exposure to multiple disciplines and diverse cultures which enriches your knowledge
  • We help students to achieve their dreams

Sandip foundation has been approved by AICTE (All India Council of Technical Education) and affiliated to Pune University which has made the degree exceptionally well regarded, respected and recognized not only in India alone but globally. Our Foundation has numerous colleges situated in the city of Nashik and Sijoul offering a wide range of courses in engineering, management, pharmacy and polytechnic. The foundation provides a system based education and training to prepare graduates for the next generation of a market. At Sandip Foundation, faculty members explore the globe to imbibe the best practices, so that students get the best environment to pursue their dreams.
